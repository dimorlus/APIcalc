  mask for selecting arguments and their order from a data string. The mask has the form 
  fitpoly("fname", "1 0", n) or fitpoly("fname", "* * 1 0", n) or fitpoly("fname", "0 * * 1", n), where

  is no template, then everything is as already done, first the first (zero), then the second.
