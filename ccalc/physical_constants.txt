=== PHYSICAL CONSTANTS FOR CALCULATOR ===

This file contains proposed two-letter physical constants to avoid conflicts with SI prefixes.

FUNDAMENTAL CONSTANTS:
======================
c0 - Speed of light in vacuum
     Value: 299792458 m/s
     Description: The speed of electromagnetic radiation in vacuum

hp - Planck constant
     Value: 6.62607015e-34 J·s
     Description: Fundamental constant relating energy to frequency

hb - Reduced Planck constant (ℏ = h/2π)
     Value: 1.054571817e-34 J·s
     Description: Planck constant divided by 2π, commonly used in quantum mechanics

gn - Gravitational constant (Newton's constant)
     Value: 6.67430e-11 m³/(kg·s²)
     Description: Constant of proportionality in Newton's law of universal gravitation

na - Avogadro constant
     Value: 6.02214076e23 mol⁻¹
     Description: Number of constituent particles per mole

kb - Boltzmann constant
     Value: 1.380649e-23 J/K
     Description: Relates average kinetic energy of particles to temperature

rg - Universal gas constant
     Value: 8.314462618 J/(mol·K)
     Description: Product of Boltzmann constant and Avogadro constant


ELECTROMAGNETIC CONSTANTS:
===========================
e0 - Electric constant (vacuum permittivity)
     Value: 8.8541878128e-12 F/m
     Description: Permittivity of free space

u0 - Magnetic constant (vacuum permeability)
     Value: 1.25663706212e-6 H/m
     Description: Permeability of free space

z0 - Characteristic impedance of vacuum
     Value: 376.730313668 Ω
     Description: Wave impedance of electromagnetic waves in vacuum


PARTICLE CONSTANTS:
===================
qe - Elementary charge
     Value: 1.602176634e-19 C
     Description: Electric charge of a proton (magnitude)

me - Electron mass
     Value: 9.1093837015e-31 kg
     Description: Rest mass of an electron

mp - Proton mass
     Value: 1.67262192369e-27 kg
     Description: Rest mass of a proton

mn - Neutron mass
     Value: 1.67492749804e-27 kg
     Description: Rest mass of a neutron

re - Classical electron radius
     Value: 2.8179403262e-15 m
     Description: Characteristic length scale for electron scattering

a0 - Bohr radius
     Value: 5.29177210903e-11 m
     Description: Most probable distance between nucleus and electron in hydrogen ground state


ASTRONOMICAL CONSTANTS:
========================
au - Astronomical unit
     Value: 1.495978707e11 m
     Description: Mean distance from Earth to Sun

ly - Light year
     Value: 9.4607304725808e15 m
     Description: Distance light travels in one Julian year

pc - Parsec
     Value: 3.0856775814914e16 m
     Description: Parallax of one arcsecond, astronomical distance unit


ADDITIONAL CONSTANTS:
=====================
ry - Rydberg constant
     Value: 10973731.568160 m⁻¹
     Description: Spectroscopic constant for atomic spectra

sb - Stefan-Boltzmann constant
     Value: 5.670374419e-8 W/(m²·K⁴)
     Description: Constant relating black body radiation to temperature


NOTES:
======
- All constants use two-letter names to avoid conflicts with SI prefixes
- Constants are lowercase to distinguish from units
- Values are from CODATA 2018 recommended values
- Will respect /UPCASE+ option for case-insensitive matching
